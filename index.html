About the School

Sishu Tirtha High School is an English-medium educational institution located on PWD Road, Silchar. Established on 16 April 1975 ( 2 BOISHAKH ), the school initially began as a Bengali-medium institution and was later upgraded to English medium to meet evolving educational needs. With a long-standing presence in the region, the school is recognized as one of the oldest private educational institutions in Silchar, committed to providing structured and value-based education.

Director's Message

MR. SAMAR BIJOY CHAKRABORTY

At Sishu Tirtha High School, we recognize education as a cornerstone for cultivating responsible, competent, and principled individuals. In addition to academic excellence, the school upholds five core values that guide the conduct and character of our students: Punctuality, Honesty, Keeping One’s Word, Discipline, and Respect. Through a structured and supportive learning environment, we are committed to fostering students who demonstrate integrity, confidence, and the ability to contribute meaningfully to society.

Founder

Lt. MRINALINI BHATTACHARJEE

Late Mrinalini Bhattacharjee was an independent and dedicated woman who envisioned education as a means to shape the future of children. With this belief, she established the institution in Silchar with the objective of providing quality education to the local community. The school was initially started at Silchar Sangeet Vidyalaya and was later shifted to its present location on PWD Road, Silchar, to support its growth and development
